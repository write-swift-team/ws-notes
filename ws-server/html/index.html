What is Write Swift Notes?

WS Notes is a service for writing educational notes online with quick and easy editing functions.

Write a draft, edit when the time comes, share the result with your friends. Over the years of interaction, our user base itself will be able to provide colleagues in writing with ready-made versions of abstracts on various topics.

You no longer need to ask your neighbor what is written on the board: ask your friend to turn on the live broadcast of the outline. Share, interact and enjoy learning!
